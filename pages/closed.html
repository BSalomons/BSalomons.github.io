Closed - Sale or Lease Transaction that has Closed

Once a transaction has closed, there isn’t anything an agent is to do at this point. It falls on the Broker to close the deal and once that occurs, the agent will receive an autogenerated email stating the deal has been closed.

When the agent receives the email, it is imperative to double check that the data entered into the Closed Deal record is accurate.

There are three reasons for this:

    Agent’s commissions are tabulated from these records. Making sure the closing date and the values are accurate ensures you are allocated your proper share. This will at some point serve as our comps database which will be shared and used for proposals.
  1. For lease transactions, it is possible to send yourself reminders on when their term is coming due so that you canpotentially get a lead out of it.
