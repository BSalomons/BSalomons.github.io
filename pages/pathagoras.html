Pathagoras

Pathagoras is an application that is installed as a Microsoft Word add-in. What this means is that the features and functionality of Microsoft Word serves as the base program and Pathagoras uses these tools for its features.

The purpose of Pathagoras is to construct and create legal documents in a convenient manner. Due to the uniqueness of every transaction, the program will prompt you with numerous questions to add or delete clauses as the situation requires. Once complete, the constructed document will have further variables to populate such as dates, names, addresses, etc.
