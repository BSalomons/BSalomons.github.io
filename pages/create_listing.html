Create a New Listing

Prerequisites:

  1. Has the client with the Listing been entered into the Salesforce? No: click here
  2. Does the underlying Property exist in the Database? How to check. No: click here
  3. Is the underlying Property information correct and current? No: click here
  4. If a Lease listing, is the Premises information correct and current? No: click here

New Record:

Two ways to create a new listing:

  1. Within the underlying Property page, on the right hand side, click “Create Listing”
  2. Choose the Listing option on the left hand side and click green button “Add Listing”

Complete the fields in the Listing page form:

  • Common Name of the Property Owner followed by a dash and the address of the underlying Property name
  • Ex: “Parkland Properties Ltd – 4919 59 Street”
  • Must follow this nomenclature
  • This is what is displayed on the front end of the website AND will form part of the URL
  • Editing and changing this field will result in a new URL on the front end and any previous shared links (from mailouts, brochures, etc.) will be broken
  • Sale Listing: choose “Seller Representation”
  • Lease Listing: choose “Landlord Representation”

At this point, click the “Save and Continue Editing” button on the right-hand side before proceeding

  • If populated with a date, the Active checkbox will be deselected if the date is in the past
  • Leave blank otherwise

  • Leave blank otherwise
  • Choose the appropriate selection from the picklist
  • If populated with a date, the Status field will default back to “Available” if the date is in the past
  • Leave blank otherwise
  • If a Sale listing, enter the sale price. If the price is to be undisclosed, then put the expected price. This does not display on the website but this field is used with the filters

At this point, click the “Save and Continue Editing” button on the right-hand side before proceeding

  • Enter the date that the listing agreement was signed
  • His the “today” button
  • Enter the expiry date that is written in the listing agreement
  • If there is an automatic renewal clause in the listing agreement, check this box
  • Enter the number of months contained in the automatic renewal clause. If month-to-month, enter 1
  • Add the various features associated with the property
  • Upload the video to the youtube account
  • Use the share button in youtube to copy the url and paste it in the applicable field

  • Add a second agent if applicable

Review all data. If good hit the “Save” button.
